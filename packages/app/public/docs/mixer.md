# Mixer

The bottom drawer holds the mixer. Click the **▾ / ▴** chevron (or
press **Cmd / Ctrl + M**) to collapse / expand. Default state and
height persist per-machine.

## Channel strip anatomy

Each strip has, top to bottom:

- **Color stripe + track name** — matches the rail / canvas head.
- **Insert FX slots** — up to 4 visible. Filled slots show the
  effect name; empty slots are dashed placeholders. Click an empty
  slot to open the plugin picker scoped to insertion on this track.
- **Sends** — sends to bus tracks. The demo's Lead + Bass send into
  the Reverb Bus.
- **Pan** — −1 (left) to +1 (right). Wheel or drag.
- **Fader** + **stereo meter** — peak + RMS with hold-line. Wheel
  or drag to change.
- **S / M pills** — Solo isolates the track; Mute silences it.
- **dB readout** — current fader value in dB (mono font).

## Master strip

The Master strip on the right is **wider** and reserves a slot for
a master limiter. The Master meter in the transport bar mirrors
its output post-fader.

## Popout

The mixer can pop into a separate window for multi-monitor work.
Click the **⤴** icon at the top of the drawer. While the popup is
open, the in-root drawer hides itself (no duplicate controls).
Closing the popup restores the drawer.
